[h1] Stackland Plus [/h1]

New Cards: Food Chest; Stick Workshop, Shed Workshop, Warehouse Workshop; Fruit Salad Workshop, Milkshake Workshop; Super Garden, Super Farm, and Super Greenhouse, which can plant multiple plants at once.

[hr][/hr]

This Mod aims to optimize repetitive actions in the game by [b] providing new cards [/b], while striving not to alter the original game logic.

[hr][/hr]

[h2]New Card: Food Chest[/h2]

A box that can store food.

[h2]New Card: Wooden Pile[/h2]

Use two wooden sticks and have a villager craft a wooden pile. It can be used to tie up an animal. In the early stages, we are no longer helpless against animals.

[h2]New Card: Food Workshop[/h2]

A workshop that produces fruit salad and milkshake.

[h2]New Card: Building Workshop[/h2]

A workshop that produces sticks, builds sheds, and warehouses. It requires human-type cards to operate.

[h3]New Card: Super Planting Technology[/h3]

Provides a super garden, super farm, and super greenhouse, producing multiple plants in one go.

[h3]New Card: Research Center[/h3]

Used to synthesize a super garden, super farm, and super greenhouse; it also upgrades a super garden to a super farm and a super farm to a super greenhouse.
